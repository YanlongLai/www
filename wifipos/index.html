WifiPos

Increase working efficiency and shorten ordering process

Traditionally waiters/waitresses have to use paper to write it down what guests ordering which takes some time if it’s a long food name (based on different language characters). WifiPos only acquires a tabslet which can be efficiently connected between front and back of house management. One-tap on the “E-table”at the front house, the back house would get an immediate response and be ready to serve on time.

Being eco-friendly with less paper consumption

It would be a tremendous expense for the paper consumption along with the growing business. By saving our living environment as well as daily expense, reduction of paper is necessary for business purpose.

Easier maintenance and Scalable extension

Wifi-POS uses wifi and is easier to maintain ordering system on daily basis than App which takes higher expenses and more time to be developed and updated across different platforms. Besides, it can be extended to be applied for the future development such as self-order system, remote-order system, etc.

Evolution of food order

In 21st century, human’s life style and habit are changing and being evolved by technology development, therefore, utilising technology is essential to flourish business.
