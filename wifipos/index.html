Table Management

  1. Support multi-platforms (e.g. Android, iOS, Windows etc.)
  2. Support multi-people using (e.g. 100 people ordering at the time)
  3. Manually add/remove tables/seats
  4. Different accounts enable different authorizations
  5. Support different food ordering modes: Waiter mode/Guest mode/Kiosk/QR Code
  6. Screen adjustable to fit different devices (phone, tablet, computer etc)
  7. Kitchen side can see food orders via both paper or device screen
  8. Sales (data) analytics
  9. Data/file export/backup
  10. Operational area can reach entire building or wifi-located area
